Process

Characteristics

The essence of the process is the execution of the program in the multiprogramming system, which is dynamically generated and dynamically dies.
 Dynamic
Any process can be executed concurrently with other processes.
 Concurrency
A process is a basic unit that can operate independently and is also an independent unit of system allocation resources and scheduling.
 Independence
Due to the interprocess constraints, the process has the intermittent nature of execution, that is, the process moves forward at its own independent and unpredictable speed Structural features: process consists of three parts: program, data, and process control block.
